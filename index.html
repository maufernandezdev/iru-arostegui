Senderos de Raza

This is one of the biggest meat companies from Neuquen, Argentina.
In this project, we digitalized all of the processes, so we can say that it is a fisical e-commerce. The idea was to eliminate the queue, you can buy it on any digital device, and the product will be delivered to your house or you can pick up what you bought 15 minutes later.

Aislaciones Patagonicas

This company focused on the manufacture of smart materials for construction, being one of the biggest producers in Argentina. In this case, I only worked on the home and products section. Each product page, the hero images, the panels, and even the icons from the “attributes” menu was fully designed by me, following the look and feel of the brand.
